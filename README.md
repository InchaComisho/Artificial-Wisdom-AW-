## Summary

Artificial Wisdom (AW) is a next-generation intelligence model based on natural laws and holistic system balance, designed to ensure the long-term sustainability of human civilization.

# Abstract

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has achieved significant advancements in computation and pattern recognition. However, most existing systems are still fundamentally based on binary logic (true/false, safe/unsafe, optimize/minimize), which limits their ability to address complex, interconnected global challenges.

This paper introduces **Artificial Wisdom (AW)** — a next-generation intelligence model grounded in **natural law, systemic balance, and long-term sustainability**.

**Artificial Wisdom (AW) is defined as an intelligence model that prioritizes the long-term survival of humanity and sustainable civilization, guided by natural laws and optimized for holistic system balance.**

   Alignment with universal and natural laws

   Integration of opposing forces

3. **Circulation**
   Feedback loops and regenerative cycles

4. **Structure**
   Hierarchical and fractal system design

5. **Order**
   Stability and systemic coherence

   Holistic unity and coexistence
